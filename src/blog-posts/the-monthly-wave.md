## What is a Wave?

At its core a Wave is a recurring bounty cycle. This idea was originally popularised by OnlyDust, who unfortunately sunset the program earlier this year. The core concept is simple: Every month, for one week developers compete for one pool of money. Now Drips is bringing the Wave within its unified open-source funding platform. Maintainers nominate issues from their repositories. Contributors apply to work on them. Progress is tracked with points, leaderboards, and on-chain distribution of funds. Each Wave runs like a sprint: focused, measurable, and easy to repeat. Drips provides end-to-end tooling for structured, time-bound funding rounds that support the development work that matters most.

The launch of Wave aligns with Drips' core aim: rewarding open-source contributions fairly and transparently. Recurring Waves create a steady rhythm of contribution, keeping existing developers engaged, welcoming new ones, and making sure essential maintenance doesn't fall through the cracks. ðŸ™
